An atlas of the tools behind technology leadership

Tooling Atlas

Leadership in a field tends to follow leadership in its tools — and the lead migrates over time. Each tool here is an instrument, machine, method, or process someone built and others picked up, mapped four ways.

The four jobs a tool does

Every tool here does one of four jobs, and they fall in order.

Measuresee what is there — a microscope, an X-ray, a way to read DNA
Modelreason about it — an equation or rule that predicts without building
Makebuild one working copy — a single transistor, engine, or molecule
Manufacturebuild a billion, cheap and identical, on a factory floor

See it, understand it, build one, build a million.
